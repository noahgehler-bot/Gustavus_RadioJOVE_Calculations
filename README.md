As apart of the Gustavus RadioJOVE research team for the summer of 2026 we have written the following programs. This program is based upon the calculations of J. P. Noyola in the research paper, Detection of Exomoons Through Observations of Radio Emissions. We have taken those calculations and made two programs to quickly calculate the sensitivity needed to see a certain exomoon and to calculate the exomoons that can be detected given some telescopes sensitivity. These calculations could help up estimate which exoplanets may house realisticly sizes exomoons. Many things about exoplanets and moons are still unknown so many assumptions are made which in turn could result in inaccurate calculations. Big thanks to our PI, Dr. Darsa Donelan, and the 2026 summer researchers.
